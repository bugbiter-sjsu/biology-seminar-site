About the Seminar

The Department of Biological Sciences is proud to host its Biology Seminar for the Spring 2026 semester. We invite biologists from academia, government, private industry and other fields to share their research with our students, faculty and staff. All members of the SJSU are invited to join us for light snacks and science.
